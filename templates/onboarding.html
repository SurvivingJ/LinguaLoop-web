Welcome to LinguaLoop

Master any language with active practice

What LinguaLoop Does

  • Improve foreign language skills through short, active exercises instead of passive study
  • Answer focused comprehension tests designed for translators, exam preparation, and serious learners
  • Each test is calibrated with an ELO rating so you are matched with challenges that fit your level
Report Button

Use the flag icon to report issues, bad translations, or suggest improvements. Your feedback helps improve test quality for everyone.

Profile

View your ELO rating, track progress over time, and see your test history. Watch how your skills improve with practice.

Language Selector

Switch the site interface language to navigate in your preferred language. More interface languages coming soon.

How Tests Work

  • Choose between listening tests (audio first, transcript shown after) or reading tests (text on screen from the start)
  • Each test has 5 multiple-choice questions with 4 options, giving you clear, targeted feedback
  • As you complete tests, LinguaLoop adapts to your level and recommends material that fits your ability
